Buying a car is a minefield, and generally ranks second on the level of financial risks after buying a house. Car Explorer helps to avoid most common mistakes.

If you are in the process of car shopping as buyer, or you are an advertising agency interested to increase productivity of your business, or you are a car manufacturer motivated by bulk car sales this version and future releases of Car Explorer reflect the vision how to make car buying and selling less risky and more enjoyable, cheaper for car buyers and with more volumes for car dealers and manufacturers.

This project was started as a grassroots entrepreneurial effort, and continues to be. All software and car descriptions on this site are free. In future releases you can expect (1) as car shopper: push technology that automatically brings on your computer or mobile device latest releases from car manufactures in compact form as CAR files, various tools including CarDiff for car comparison, (2) as car advertisement agency: a compact format for description of the cars you are marketing compatible with Car Explorer, (3) as carmaker: an integration with Car Server, direct marketing and sales.
