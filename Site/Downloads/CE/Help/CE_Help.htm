Concept

Car Explorer is designed to be a convenient inventory of all major world cars so that selecting your dream car is easy and efficient, and you can avoid most common mistakes, in particular in terms of price you pay for a car.

Car Explorer makes use of CAR files that are car descriptions in the XCD format. It is the central feature of the software. One CAR file describes all cars for one Make (automaker) and one Year. For example, the Audi 2013 CAR file (Audi_2013.car) contains all quick specs (including pictures) for Audi 2013 cars available in North America.

It means if you need to know about all 2013 cars from Audi you need to have only one file. At the moment you can download all specs in one ZIP file for the last 5 years, or use Car Explorer download option to download CAR file of your interest. This feature is supposed to be automated (Car Explorer would automatically download car specs when available).

The car descriptions are free, and will be free of charge. Hopefully car sites, car dealers, and automakers will be interested to have car descriptions in XCD format published on their sites or bundled with Car Explorer as marketing leads, and help to make the list of CAR files for every year complete (use these instructions to make or edit CAR files).

Car Explorer is a desktop application. An abridged version for handheld gadgets is planned (investors are welcome).


Benefits

Car Explorer is a promising alternative to the Internet sites that offer car descriptions for several reasons:

  1. Sites typically provide information about one or a few automakers. It means options to compare cars from different automakers are not available or limited, and even cannot be implemented.
  2. Surfing online for your dream car can be irksome and frustrating, handicapped by slow connections (car sites love flash!).
  3. Sites have poor persistence models or don't have options to store preferences at all, so it is difficult or not possible at all to keep track of your favorite cars, memorize entry points etc (by the way that's the reason why web versions of office products such as Word and Excel are not popular).
  4. To our knowledge no one previously tried to describe all world cars in a uniform format that provides quick specs for all cars released by a specific automaker in a specific year.

With Car Explorer you have instant access to quick car specs in XCD format, can instantly sort and analyze those across any number of different automakers, plus you have a single entry point, and can keep track of your favorites in an instant and convenient manner.

Search

The search view is the default view of Car Explorer.

The Search option implies that you have CAR files on your computer. Since v1.02, Car Explorer comes with samples of CAR files. Having information about all major automakers is easy since the size of car data for the 40-50 major world cars manufactures (quick specs plus pictures) for the last 5 years is 100-150 MB, quite small even for a mobile device. To enable extensive search please download free 5-Year CAR Pack or you can use the Download Collection menu option in Car Explorer to view available CAR files and make downloads one-by-one.

You can search cars by Year, Type, Price, Fuel or with any combination of these parameters. Notice that it is frequently more convenient to find a car by sorting list view in the right pane starting from a category (All, Year, Country, Make, Favortites) in the left pane.

View

You can view car descriptions as a list in the right pane starting from a category (All, Year, Country, Make, Favorites) in the left pane. Click on any column to sort data in ascending or descending order.

When you click on a CAR file in the left pane, Car Explorer provides a list of cars plus two detail views in the bottom for the selected car (pictures and specs). Use the Toggle View toolbar option to toggle between list view and icon view.


Sort and Analyze

Sorting the list of cars across all major carmakers, with any car detail (Price, Length, Width, Height, Fuel Type, Engine Power etc) as a sort parameter, with instant results is a unique and powerful feature of Car Explorer. Start from any category (All, Year, Country, Make, Favorites) in the left pane, and click any column in the right pane to sort data in ascending or descending order.


Favorites

You can keep track of your car research during one session or between sessions by using Favorites.

Simply drag-and-drop a car from the right pane (list view or icon view) to the left pane. The car will be stored under Favorites root. You can store a car favorite under a specific default folder (Sedan, Convertible, SUV, Green, Sport etc) by making drag-and-drop onto this folder, or you can create your own folders (up to 2 levels under Favorites root).

You can export favorites from any folder to CSV format, and work with it in Excel


Working with Individual Pictures

Can you view, save, or print a picture of your favorite car? Yes, easy. Double-click a car in the list view or icon view in the right pane. This opens the picture viewer integrated with Windows. Now you can save, transform, print, set as desktop background and so on.


Download Car Descriptions

As mentioned in Concept, Car Explorer is a viewer of CAR files. To make use of the program at least one CAR file should be available locally (after installation there are 3 CAR files available). All CAR files are free and can be downloaded either:

  • One-by-one within Car Explorer (Tools/Download Collections)
  • You can save time by downloading all CAR files for the last 5 years (Tools/Download Collections and click "Download 5-Year CAR Pack" button). Unzip CAR files into the current Cars Folder (see location in File/Set Cars Folder...)

It is planned to have automated downloads of CAR files upon availability in the future releases.
